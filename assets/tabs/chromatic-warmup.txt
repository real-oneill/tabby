CHROMATIC WARMUP
Practice exercise - 1 2 3 4 across the neck

Play one note per beat. Move up one fret
each time you reach the high E string.

e|-------------------------------1-2-3-4-|
B|-----------------------1-2-3-4---------|
G|---------------1-2-3-4-----------------|
D|-------1-2-3-4-------------------------|
A|---1-2-3-4-----------------------------|
E|-1-2-3-4-------------------------------|

Then come back down:

E|-4-3-2-1-------------------------------|
A|---------4-3-2-1-----------------------|
D|-----------------4-3-2-1---------------|
G|-------------------------4-3-2-1-------|
B|---------------------------------4-3-2-|
e|---------------------------------------|

Start slow. Keep it even. Build speed.
